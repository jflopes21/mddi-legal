Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the MDDI application ("the app", "we") handles information when automating the creation and publishing of short videos to the accounts you connect. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Data we access

  • Account authorization (OAuth): when you connect a YouTube and/or TikTok account, we receive an access/refresh token that allows us to publish videos on your behalf to the accounts you authorize. We never receive your password.
  • Public video data: public metadata (titles, IDs, durations and transcriptions) from the channels you configure to monitor, used to generate the clips.
  • Public metrics: views, likes and comments of published videos, used to display performance.

2. How we use the data

Data is used solely to create and publish short videos to the accounts you connect and to display performance metrics. We do not sell, rent or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Storage and security

Access tokens are stored securely (encrypted) and used only for the operations you request. You can revoke access at any time in the security settings of your Google account (myaccount.google.com/permissions) or TikTok account.

4. Third-party services

The app relies on third-party APIs to function: YouTube Data API (Google), TikTok API, and Google Gemini (for text analysis). Use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Retention and deletion

You may request deletion of your data and removal of stored tokens at any time by contacting us at the email below.
